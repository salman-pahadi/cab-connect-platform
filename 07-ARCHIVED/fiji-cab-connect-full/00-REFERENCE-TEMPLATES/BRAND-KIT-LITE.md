## üí° WHY THIS WORKS

### **Benefits:**

1. **Speed:** 45 min vs 3 hours
2. **Reduces client friction:** Don't need answers they don't have
3. **Professional defaults:** Based on industry standards
4. **Show, don't ask:** Easier for clients to react than create
5. **Upsell opportunity:** FULL workshop as premium add-on
6. **Protects you:** Content restrictions always applied
7. **Realistic:** Works with what 90% of clients actually provide

### **Client Psychology:**

**Bad:** "I need 93 decisions from you before I start"  
**Good:** "Give me your domain and industry, I'll handle the rest professionally"

**Bad:** "What colors do you want?"  
**Good:** "I used professional blue (trust) + green (growth) for tech companies. Want to see alternatives?"

Clients don't know what they want until they see it. **Show, then adjust.**
